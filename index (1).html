Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really too fun for our first trip?

▼
Absolutely! Family Trip Splitter is designed to be intuitive and fun. It takes less than a minute to set up your first trip and start adding expenses.

Can we use it when traveling different currencies?

▼
Yes! Our app supports multiple currencies and automatically converts everything to a base currency for easy settlement.

Do all family members need the app?

▼
One person can manage the trip and add expenses for everyone. Others can download the app to see their portion anytime.

Is my financial secure?

▼
Your data is encrypted and secure. We never store payment information. We only track expenses and calculate balances.

How do I invite family members?

▼
Simply share the trip code with your family. They can join by entering the code in their app.

Can I edit an expense after it's made?

▼
Yes, you can edit or delete expenses anytime. All calculations update instantly.

What if a transaction hasn't transferred?

▼
Our app calculates who owes whom. You can settle up through any payment method - Venmo, PayPal, or cash.
